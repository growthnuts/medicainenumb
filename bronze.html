PRODUCT DETAILS

THE WORKHORSE

MediCaine Bronze is our flagship triple-agent BLT formulation — the most widely used compound numbing formula in medical aesthetics and dermatology. Three anesthetic agents attack pain signals through different pathways simultaneously, delivering fast-onset, reliable numbing that your patients and staff can count on.

HOW IT WORKS

Benzocaine provides immediate surface-level relief on contact. Lidocaine penetrates deeper tissue layers for sustained numbness. Tetracaine extends the anesthetic window with its long half-life. Phenylephrine constricts blood vessels to reduce bleeding and keep the agents localized longer.

  • Laser treatments and IPL
  • Microneedling and PRP
  • Minor dermatological procedures
