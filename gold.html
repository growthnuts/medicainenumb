PRODUCT DETAILS

MAXIMUM STRENGTH

MediCaine Gold is the pinnacle of compound numbing technology. Four distinct anesthetic agents — Benzocaine, Lidocaine, Tetracaine, and Prilocaine — each attacking pain through a different mechanism, combined with Phenylephrine for vasoconstriction. This is what you reach for when the procedure demands absolute numbing confidence.

HOW IT WORKS

Benzocaine numbs on contact for instant surface relief. Lidocaine drives deep through tissue layers. Tetracaine provides an extended anesthetic window. Prilocaine adds a fourth mechanism of action for areas that resist traditional triple-agent formulas. Phenylephrine constricts blood vessels to maximize agent concentration at the application site.

BEST FOR

  • Extensive laser resurfacing (CO2, Erbium)
  • Full-face procedures and large treatment areas
  • Patients with high pain sensitivity
  • Surgical excisions and biopsies
  • Any procedure where maximum numbing is non-negotiable

OINTMENT ONLY

MediCaine Gold is formulated exclusively as an ointment. The occlusive ointment base creates a sealed barrier over the skin, maximizing absorption and keeping all four agents concentrated at the application site for the strongest possible effect.
