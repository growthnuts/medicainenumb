DEEP PENETRATION

MediCaine Silver concentrates on what matters most — depth and duration. With Lidocaine at a full 30% and Tetracaine at 7%, this two-agent powerhouse delivers the highest individual concentrations of any MediCaine formulation. Fewer agents, maximum potency per agent.
