Questions

Frequently Asked

Is a prescription required? +
Yes. All MediCaine Numb™ formulations are compounded medications that require a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider. These are not over-the-counter products. Your prescribing provider will determine the appropriate formulation and strength based on the intended procedure and patient needs.
Are these FDA-approved products? +
MediCaine Numb™ formulations are compounded in an FDA-registered, GMP-certified facility. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved products; however, they are prepared under FDA oversight in compliance with applicable compounding regulations (Section 503A/503B of the FD&C Act). Each formulation uses USP-grade ingredients and follows strict quality control protocols.
What does hypoallergenic base mean? +
Every MediCaine Numb™ formulation is compounded using a hypoallergenic base that is free from parabens, fragrances, dyes, and latex — common irritants that can cause adverse skin reactions. While the base is formulated to minimize irritation, patients with known sensitivities to any local anesthetic agent (lidocaine, benzocaine, tetracaine, or prilocaine) should inform their prescribing provider before use.
What procedures are these used for? +
MediCaine Numb™ formulations are used across the full range of aesthetic procedures including microneedling, RF microneedling (e.g., Morpheus8), dermal fillers, Botox injections, laser hair removal, CO2 laser resurfacing, chemical peels, PRP treatments, PDO threads, tattoo and permanent makeup application, and tattoo laser removal. Your provider will select the appropriate strength tier for each procedure.
What is the shelf life? +
Cream formulations have a 30-day beyond-use date (BUD) from the date of compounding. Ointment formulations have a 180-day BUD. For high-volume practices, we recommend ointment formulations for extended stability. Some maximum-strength formulations (those containing prilocaine) require refrigeration. Storage instructions are included with every order.
How do the strength tiers work? +
MediCaine Numb™ offers formulations rated from 5/10 (mild, standard BLT) to 10/10 (highest intensity, MAX Strength BLT Plus). Standard formulations are suited for mildly painful procedures like laser hair removal. Extra-strength formulations (7/10) cover fillers and microneedling. Maximum-strength formulations (9–10/10) are designed for the most intensive procedures like RF microneedling, CO2 lasers, and PDO threads. Contact us for the complete formulary.
