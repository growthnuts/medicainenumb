FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

What is MediCaine compound numbing cream?+
MediCaine is a professional-grade compound numbing cream and ointment used by medical practices, dermatologists, and aesthetic providers. Our formulations include Bronze (BLT), Silver (LT), and Gold (BLT+). All products are compounded fresh per order in the USA and require a valid prescription.
What sizes and packaging are available?+
Available in 6 sizes: 30g, 50g, 100g, 150g, 200g, and 454g. Every size comes in airless pump or jar packaging.
How fast does it work?+
15-25 minutes depending on formulation, compared to the 30-45 minute industry standard. Bronze and Gold tiers onset in approximately 15 minutes. Silver tier in approximately 20 minutes.
Do I need a prescription?+
Yes. All MediCaine products are prescription-only compound medications. A valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider is required before fulfillment.
Cream vs ointment?+
Cream has a lighter texture with easy absorption. Ointment provides a thicker barrier with maximum skin contact. Bronze and Silver come in cream or ointment. Gold is ointment only for maximum potency.
Airless pump vs jar?+
Airless pump delivers precise, consistent doses with hygienic no-contact dispensing and zero waste. Jar provides traditional easy access. Both available for all sizes.
